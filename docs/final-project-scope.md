# Final Project Scope - Hobby
![Magic The Gathering](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/a/a9/Magic_The_Gathering_Commander_2015_logo.jpg)
## **MTG Commander Beginner's Guide** - How to play Magic The Gathering's (MTG) Casual Game Format
## **Project Purpose**
### Why are we doing this project?
To create an easy-to-understand informative guide for beginner **Magic: The Gathering** players who are interested in learning the commander game format.
## **Description**
### How would you describe this project?
This project will cover the following key rules of commander:
 1. Life points start at 40, 100-card deck limit, standar color identity rules
 2. How to cast and move commanders between turns
 3. How to play the 4-player game and include multiplayer turn phases
 4. Win conditions and standard life reductions
## **Desired Results**
### What outcomes must be achieved to be successful?
Enable beginner MTG players to have the ability to play a 4-player commander game from **_start to finish_** within minutes of studying the guide.
## **Exclusions**
### What should **NOT** be included?
Competitive or complex rules for the commander format as well as rules from other multiplayer game formats should be left out of the project.
## **Feedback Loops**
### Who needs to provide feedback?
Beginner players, experienced players and certified game judges should be the main sources of feedback.
## **People Resources**
### Who are the key members of this project?
 * Project Manager - Myself
 * Graphic Designers
 * Editors/Proofreaders
 * MTG Judges
## **Priorities**
### How would your prioritize constraints? (1=most important )
 1. Quality
 2. Time
 3. Budget

The guide must be simple enough for new beginner players to understand within minutes of reading the guide while remaining relevant to the current state of the game.
## **Time**
### When is the project due?
End of the school year
## **Quality**
### How will we know when the project is done?
Beginner players can identify cards and understand their functions as well as having the ability to manage their **_command zone_**.
## **Budget**
### What is the budget?
$2,000,000
## **Acceptance Criteria**
### Who needs to sign off on the project?
The guide must be approved by **experienced** commander judges for accuracy.
